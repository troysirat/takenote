Simple Shaker

Materials

  1. A closeable can or easter egg.
  2. Either rice or noodles.
  3. Tape.

Instructions

  1. Open can.
  2. Poor rice/noodles into can.
  3. Close can.
  4. Seal the can by adding tape around the opening.
  5. Shake it.

Straw Flute

Materials

  1. A good amount of straws. Preferable 7-10.
  2. Tape.
  3. Scissors.
  4. Permanate marker.

Instructions

  1. Lay straws flat on the table making sure that they are all lined up at one side.
  2. Mark the straws diagnally to make different lengths.
  3. Cut each straw to the marked length.
  4. Tape the straws in order from biggest to smallest or smallest to biggest. Tape in pairs to make the flute more sturdy.
  5. Finish by taping the pair together in order until there's a complete set.
  6. Flute on.

Ultimate Kazoo

Materials

  1. Toilet or paper towel roll.
  2. Wax paper.
  3. Scissors.
  4. Something elastic like a rubber band.

Instructions

  1. Tear off a piece of wax paper that is large enough to fit around the opening and a little bit below.
  2. Rap it around one of the ends of the roll.
  3. Secure using the elastic band making sure that the wax paper it tight up against the roll.
  4. Take the scissors and carefully poke a hole right benieth the elastic band.
  5. Hum away.
